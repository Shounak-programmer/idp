# 🚦 Smart Traffic Management & Hornless Vehicle Communication System

## 🌍 Overview
This project aims to revolutionize road traffic systems in India by combining **AI**, **IoT**, and **innovative communication technologies** to improve **ambulance response time** and **reduce noise pollution**.  
It is an interdisciplinary project developed by second-year B.Tech students of **Adamas University**.

---

## 🚑 Module 1: Ambulance Priority Traffic Management System

### 🔍 Problem
Thousands of patients die each year due to ambulances being delayed in traffic, as traditional systems cannot prioritize emergency vehicles.

### 💡 Solution
We propose an **AI- and IoT-based system** that:
- Detects ambulances using **live camera feeds and GPS**.
- Communicates with **traffic signals within a 10 km radius** to automatically give green priority.
- Displays **“Ambulance Approaching – Clear the Way”** alerts on signals.
- Uses **real-time image processing** to select the **least congested route** to the hospital.

### ⚙️ Technologies
- **Python**, **OpenCV**, **YOLOv8** for vehicle and traffic detection  
- **ESP32 / Raspberry Pi** for IoT modules  
- **GPS + GSM** for ambulance tracking  
- **Firebase / MQTT** for communication  
- **Google Maps API / OpenStreetMap** for route planning

---

## 🚗 Module 2: Smart Hornless Communication System

### 🔍 Problem
Constant honking in Indian cities causes massive **noise pollution**, stress, and hearing issues.

### 💡 Solution
We introduce a **silent, non-audible signaling system** to replace car horns:
- Vehicles communicate via **Bluetooth / Ultrasonic / IR signals**.
- When a driver presses the horn button, the car sends a **digital alert** instead of sound.
- Nearby cars receive this alert and display the **approaching vehicle’s direction** (radar-style).
- For users who prefer sound cues, **3D spatial audio** (simulated via headphones) reproduces realistic honk directionality without external noise.

### ⚙️ Technologies
- **ESP32 / Ultrasonic / IR transceivers** for V2V (Vehicle-to-Vehicle) signaling  
- **Python / MATLAB** for signal processing  
- **Unity Audio Engine / PyAudio** for 3D spatial sound simulation  

---

## 🧩 System Integration
Both modules work together on a **shared communication network**.  
- Ambulances can broadcast their presence to other vehicles and traffic lights through the same system.  
- Traffic lights, vehicles, and emergency systems coordinate in real-time to minimize delays and improve road safety.

## 🧠 Methodology
1. **Research & Planning** – Analyze existing traffic systems, ambulance delays, and communication methods.  
2. **AI Model Development** – Train YOLO model for vehicle detection and congestion analysis.  
3. **IoT Prototype Setup** – Connect GPS/GSM modules to ESP32; simulate smart signals.  
4. **Software Integration** – Build backend + dashboard for real-time monitoring.  
5. **Hornless Communication Simulation** – Design vehicle-to-vehicle silent signaling and audio simulation.  
6. **Testing & Evaluation** – Test integration with mock traffic scenarios.
